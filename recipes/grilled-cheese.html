Grilled Cheese

grilled cheese sandwich

The comfort food of a childs soul. Easy to make and even easier to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 sourdough slices
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 2 slices of swiss
  • 2 slices of chedder

Steps

  1. heat pan over med heat add 1 pad of butter
  2. butter one side of each slice of bread (1 tbsp each)
  3. place each slice of bread butter-side-down in pan
  4. layer the cheeses
  5. once the cheese has started melting put the slices of bread together (still in the pan)
  6. let one side sit for about a minute, flip and let sit another minute
