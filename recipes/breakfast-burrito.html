Breakfast Burrito

vegetarian burrito

Burritos have endless possibilties when it comes
to preparing them. Think of the tortilla as an empty suitcase ready to fill.

Ingredients

  • large flour tortilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 vegetarian sausage patties (we use MorningStar)
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • pepper jack cheese
  • sour cream
  • sliced avocado

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. heat pan to med high with a little butter or oil
  3. beat eggs with taco seasoning until mixed
  4. cook veggie sausage (about 5min until done)
  5. cook eggs until almost fluffy
  6. warm tortilla (makes it easier to fold)
  7. place eggs onto the tortilla and crumble veggie sausage on top
  8. sprinkle with cheese and add sour cream/ avocado
  9. fold into burrito and place in oven for 3-5
